Regular Verbs

Regular Verbs are those verbs that have an "-ed" ending when they are conjugated in the Past Simple Tense

Here you have some examples:

Verb (in infinitive) Past Simple Tense
Work Worked
Live Lived
Need Needed
Jump Jumped
Walk Walked

Notice that in the Past Simple Tense, the verbs had en "ed"ending. That means, that the verb in conjugated in past and it also means that it is a regular verb.
