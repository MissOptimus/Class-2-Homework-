Irregular Verbs

Unlike the Regular Verbs, Irregular Verbs do not have an specific ending when they are conjugated in the Past Simple Tense. The only way of differentiate both types of verbs is learning the Irregular Verbs by heart. Fortunately for you, Irregular Verbs are less than the Regular Verbs.

Here you have some examples of Irregular Verbs:

Verb (in infinitive) Past Simple Tense
Teach Taught
Cut Cut
Buy Bought
Feel Felt
Eat Ate
Understand Understood

Notice that in the Past Simple Tense, the verbs did not have a specific ending. Some Irregular Verbschange completelly, some others change only one letter, some other do not change at all. That's the reason why you need to learn them by heart.
